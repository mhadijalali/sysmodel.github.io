Benjamin Deboer, Ph.D Student - Potential Motors' Research Assistant


Ben DeBoer is a mechanical engineer pursuing a Ph.D. at the University of New Brunswick, obtaining his undergraduate and master's from Ontario Tech. Ben has automotive industry experience, along with previous research topics ranging from the design and control of assistive exoskeletons to simulations of mechanical systems. The focus of Ben's Ph.D work is modelling, control, and simulation of semi-autonomous off-road vehicles.
